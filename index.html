About JIDO

JIDO is the premier Industrial Automation club at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bengaluru, dedicated to fostering innovation and expertise in the field of automation. Our mission is to empower students with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies, bridging the gap between academia and industry. Through workshops, hackathons, industrial visits, projects, and collaboration with industry experts, we aim to equip members with the skills needed to thrive in the evolving landscape of industrial automation.
